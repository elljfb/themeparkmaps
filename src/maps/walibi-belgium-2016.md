**Walibi Belgium**, located in Wavre, is one of Belgium's largest and most famous theme parks. This 2016 park map captures the destination at an exciting moment of modernization, marked by the introduction of highly unique attractions and a refresh of its classic layout.

![Walibi Belgium Map 2016](/assets/img/maps/walibi_belgium_map_2016.jpg)

### Major Thrills and Unique Coasters

The 2016 map showcases Walibi Belgium's strong lineup of roller coasters and thrill rides:

- **Pulsar** — newly opened in 2016, this groundbreaking Mack Rides Power Splash launched riders back and forth over a massive splash pool, reaching speeds of 100 km/h.
- **Werewolf (Loup-Garou)** — the park's classic wooden roller coaster, providing classic wooden coaster thrills and airtime.
- **Cobra** — a classic Boomerang shuttle coaster that sends riders forward and backward through loops.
- **Psyké Underground** — a launched shuttle coaster themed in pitch darkness, originally opening in 1982 as Sirocco.
- **Vampire** — a Vekoma Suspended Looping Coaster (SLC) featuring multiple inversions.
- **Dalton Terror** — the park's signature freefall tower, dominating the skyline and offering breathtaking views.

### An Evolving Theme Park

In 2016, Walibi Belgium was known for its friendly mascot (the orange kangaroo Walibi) and its mix of major roller coasters and family areas like the Walibi Playland. This guide map highlights a park that successfully balances intense thrill rides for teenagers and adults with themed water rides (like Radja River and Flash Back) and kids' play areas.

It serves as a fantastic snapshot of the park just before its massive multi-year master plan began transforming the various areas of the park into themed lands like Exotic World and Karma World.
