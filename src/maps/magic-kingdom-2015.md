This 2015 guide map of the **Magic Kingdom** showcases the park following the largest expansion in its history: **New Fantasyland**. Completed in phases between 2012 and 2014, the expansion doubled the size of Fantasyland, introducing richer storytelling, new dining options, and modernized attractions.

![Magic Kingdom Map 2015](/assets/img/maps/magic_kingdom_map_2015.png)

### The Era of New Fantasyland

The 2015 map highlights several new additions that redefined the park’s layout:

- **Seven Dwarfs Mine Train** — the crowning jewel of the expansion, a family coaster featuring pivoting mine cars that sway back and forth.
- **Under the Sea ~ Journey of The Little Mermaid** — a musical ride-through inside Prince Eric's Castle.
- **Be Our Guest Restaurant** — a highly themed dining experience inside the Beast's Castle, allowing guests to dine in the Ballroom, West Wing, or Castle Gallery.
- **Storybook Circus** — a rethemed area featuring the double Dumbo the Flying Elephant ride, Barnstormer coaster, and the Casey Jr. Splash 'N' Junction.

### Other 2015 Features

Beyond the Fantasyland expansion, the 2015 map documents the park at a time of modern updates:

- The use of **MagicBands** and the FastPass+ system, which changed how guests planned their days.
- The silhouette of **Space Mountain** in Tomorrowland and **Big Thunder Mountain** / **Splash Mountain** in Frontierland continuing to anchor the park's thrill options.

This guide map reflects the perfect blend of Magic Kingdom's classic 1971 charm and the major investments of the early 2010s, capturing a modern look at the world's most popular theme park.
