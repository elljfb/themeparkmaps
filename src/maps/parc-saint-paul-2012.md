Located in the Oise department of northern France, **Parc Saint Paul** is known for being a family-friendly amusement park filled with quirky, unique attractions. This 2012 guide map highlights the park’s compact layout and wide array of rides designed to appeal to families and children, all set in a scenic, lakeside environment.

![Parc Saint Paul Map 2012](/assets/img/maps/parc_saint_paul_map_2012.jpg)

### Quaint Charm and Unique Rides

In 2012, Parc Saint Paul was known for its highly accessible and fun selection of rides:

- **Woody Navigation** — a relaxing, family boat ride through water channels.
- **Le Wild Train** — a signature, airtime-packed Pax family coaster that has long been a favorite for enthusiasts visiting the park.
- **La Tour Descente Extreme** — a drop tower offering thrilling views of the surrounding countryside before a freefall back to the ground.
- **Le Mirage** — a family roller coaster offering twists and turns overlooking the central lake.
- **Various family attractions** — including slides, carousels, and animal encounters that gave the park its community, picnic-friendly vibe.

This 2012 map captures the simple, joyful essence of a regional European theme park, showcasing a wide variety of rides and show options that have made Parc Saint Paul a popular getaway for families in northern France.
